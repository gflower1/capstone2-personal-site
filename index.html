Summary

As the Project Director at Mike Hooks, LLC, I lead the Project Management, Quality Control, and Surveying departments, driving excellence and innovation with over a decade of experience in the construction and dredging industries.

In 2014, I founded Triton Ventures, LLC, initially providing administrative services for construction projects and later expanding into website design and hosting in 2019.

My expertise spans identifying organizational inefficiencies, mentoring project managers, and streamlining processes across departments.

Committed to leveraging technology for operational efficiency, I pursued web development in 2024 to enhance service delivery for web clients and create software solutions that support my role at Mike Hooks.

My diverse skill set and entrepreneurial spirit underpin my dedication to continuous improvement and innovation in all professional endeavors.

About Me

Efficiency. Development. Innovation.

I currently serve as the Project Director at Mike Hooks, LLC, where I lead the Project Management, Quality Control, and Surveying departments. In addition to my leadership at Mike Hooks, I founded my own company, Triton Ventures, LLC, in 2014. Initially providing administrative services for construction projects, and expanding into website design and hosting in 2019, my broad expertise includes identifying organizational inefficiencies, mentoring project managers, and coordinating between departments to streamline processes.

I am deeply committed to leveraging technology to enhance operational efficiency, which has led me to pursue web development in 2024. This new venture aims to better serve my web clients and create software solutions that support my primary role at Mike Hooks and explore other emerging opportunities. My diverse skill set and entrepreneurial spirit drive my passion for continuous improvement and innovation in all my professional endeavors.
